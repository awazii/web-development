One Piece

One Piece follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the ability to stretch like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. Luffy dreams of finding the legendary treasure known as the “One Piece” and becoming the King of the Pirates. He forms a crew called the Straw Hat Pirates, including swordsman Roronoa Zoro, navigator Nami, sharpshooter Usopp, and chef Sanji. Together, they sail the Grand Line, facing powerful enemies and uncovering ancient mysteries. The series is renowned for its rich world-building, diverse characters, and epic battles. Created by Eiichiro Oda, it has become the best-selling manga series in history
